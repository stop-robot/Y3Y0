Q2.
 I have many departments in my company, what can I do to send an E-mail to all the employees in the same department?
Ans.
 You can create a group E-mail for a certain department and set the employees under the same department members of the group E-mail. So, when you want to send out an E-mail to a certain department, you can set the group E-mail as a receiver, and then hiBox will send the E-mail to all the members of the group E-mail. Please log into the manager interface and follow the route below to finish the setup: “system management”à“group E-mail”

Website Problems
Q1.
 Is it possible that E-mails from senders I know are put into the quarantine area? If it happens, what should I do?
Ans.
 If E-mails fall into the criteria we set for advertisement mails, then they will be guided to the quarantine area. The system will notify you of the spam on a daily basis, so you can actually check to see what E-mails are put into that area. If you want to send certain emails back into your mailbox, all you need to do is click “release” to release it. You can also just go to the quarantine area directly to check the quarantined E-mails and release them if necessary. The E-mails guided to the quarantine area will be kept for 7 days, so do check your S quarantine area regularly, in case important E-mails might be deleted by the system.
Q2.
 I receive junk mail that I don’t want to receive. What can I do to filter them out?
Ans.
 You can choose to delete unwanted E-mails directly through the personal filtering function provided by hiBox. Here is the route: log into webmailàclick “item setting”à”E-mails”àE-mail receiving rule setting”
Q3.
 What is a “public address book”? What should I do to set it?
Ans.
 If your E-mail address contains your corporate Domain Name, then a manager can create a contact list that can be used by everyone inside the company through the manager interface. So, whenever you are writing an E-mail, you can just click the public address book; then it will show you all the contacts on the list for you to choose from.
Q4.
 What is the “corporate address book”? What should I do to set it up correctly?
Ans.
 There is no need to do any setting up regarding the “corporate address book”. As long as your E-mail address has your corporate Domain Name in it, when you create a new E-mail and select the “corporate address book”, then it will show you all the contacts with the same corporate Domain Name.
