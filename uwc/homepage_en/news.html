which can be exploited by hackers to crack encrypted information.

2011.06.29 
			
hiBox would like to remind you again of the importance of choosing a strong password to 
prevent password hacking. Some hiBox users become the victims of hackers because of using 
weak or easy-to-guess passwords. Easily guessed passwords that are too short; too simple; 
or that contain common words. A strong password should consist of at least eight characters 
that are a combination of letters, numbers and symbols. Strong passwords also do not contain 
words that can be found in a dictionary or parts of your email account. We strongly recommend 
that you change your password immediately if you have a weak one.

Thank you!
